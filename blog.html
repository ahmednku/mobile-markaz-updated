REALME C21 REVIEW: GEOMETRIC ART DESIGN IN BUDGET

23-Sep-2023


Welcome to our review of the Realme C21, the latest budget smartphone from Realme. If you're looking for a budget phone that still delivers solid performance, the Realme C21 is definitely worth considering.

Let's start with the design. The Realme C21 has a sleek and stylish look, with a smooth finish on the back and a slim profile that feels great in your hand. It's available in two colors: Cross Black and Cross Blue. The phone features a 6.5-inch HD+ display with a waterdrop notch at the top for the front-facing camera.

Under the hood, the Realme C21 is powered by a MediaTek Helio G35 processor, which is paired with 3GB of RAM and 32GB of internal storage. It also has a microSD card slot, which allows you to expand the storage up to 256GB. This is definitely not the most powerful smartphone on the market, but it can handle most basic tasks such as browsing the web, using social media apps, and streaming videos without any lag.

The Realme C21 has a triple-camera setup on the back, which consists of a 13-megapixel primary camera, a 2-megapixel macro camera, and a 2-megapixel depth sensor. The camera performs decently well in good lighting conditions, but struggles in low light situations. The front-facing camera is a 5-megapixel shooter, which takes satisfactory selfies.

One of the standout features of the Realme C21 is its battery life. The phone has a massive 5,000mAh battery, which can easily last you through a full day of moderate usage. It also supports reverse charging, which means you can use it to charge other devices.

In terms of software, the Realme C21 runs on Android 10 with Realme UI 1.0 on top. It comes with some pre-installed apps that you might not need, but these can easily be uninstalled. Realme UI offers a clean and intuitive interface with some useful features like screen recording and a dark mode.

Overall, the Realme C21 is a great budget smartphone that offers decent performance, a sleek design, and excellent battery life. It's not the most powerful phone out there, but it gets the job done for most basic tasks. If you're on a tight budget, the Realme C21 is definitely worth considering.
